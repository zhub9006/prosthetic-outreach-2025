# Clinical Trials: Affordable Prosthetic Solutions

Relevant clinical trials identified for the prosthetic outreach initiative, focusing on cost-effective and low-cost prosthetic devices and assistive technology.

## Summary of Key Trials

| NCT ID | Title | Status | Cost Focus | Key Finding |
|--------|-------|--------|------------|-------------|
| NCT05320211 | 3D-Printed Hand Orthoses for Chronic Hand Conditions | COMPLETED | ✅ Production time & cost comparison | 3D-printed orthoses significantly reduce production time and cost vs. conventional custom orthoses |
| NCT03965065 | COPERA: Sutureless vs Standard Valve Prostheses | UNKNOWN / RECRUITING | ✅ Cost-effectiveness analysis | Comparing hemodynamic & cost outcomes between sutureless and standard biological aortic prostheses (350 patients, 6 hospitals Spain) |
| NCT02652403 | Simplified vs Conventional Complete Denture Fabrication | COMPLETED | ✅ Direct cost analysis | Simplified technique reduces manufacturing costs while maintaining quality of life outcomes |
| NCT06546813 | ATTAIN: ICT Assistive Technology in Rehab Pathways | RECRUITING | ⚠️ Accessibility & cost in assessment | Evaluating cost of assistive technology access through standardized outcome assessment (50 pts, Milan) |
| NCT04446078 | PEEK-Acrylic Resin Prostheses for Full Arch Rehabilitation | COMPLETED | ⚠️ Material cost evaluation | Long-term evaluation of PEEK prosthetic material (5-year study, 37 patients, Portugal) |

## Notes for Outreach Teams

- **3D printing (NCT05320211)** is the most directly relevant to affordable limb prosthetics — it demonstrates cost-effective production via scanning & printing
- **COPERA (NCT03965065)** is the only trial with explicit cost-effectiveness comparison among prosthetic devices currently recruiting
- The **simplified technique study (NCT02652403)** offers a methodology template for reducing prosthetic fabrication costs

## How to Use This File

1. Reach out to trial coordinators (see full study details on ClinicalTrials.gov using NCT IDs)
2. Identify applicable low-cost manufacturing methods to your prosthetic supply chain
3. Share outcome measures (QUEST, D-QUEST, EQ-5D-5L) for community prosthetics assessment
4. Explore 3D scanning/printing workflows as scalable production alternatives
