# 2025 Prosthetic Outreach Initiative

Coordination hub for volunteer tasks across our prosthetic outreach initiative teams. Organize tasks, track progress, and collaborate on affordable prosthetic solutions.

## Project Goals

1. **Research & Development** — Identify and evaluate affordable prosthetic technologies and materials (3D printing, cost reduction)
2. **Coordination** — Streamline volunteer task management across Chicago, Boston, and Atlanta teams
3. **Outreach** — Plan and execute community engagement events in all three cities
4. **Distribution** — Organize collection, refurbishment, and delivery of prosthetic devices
