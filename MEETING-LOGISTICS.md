# Meeting Logistics

## Suggested Meeting Hub: Pittsburgh, PA

Pittsburgh is geographically central to all three team cities and offers excellent venues for coordination meetings.

### Why Pittsburgh?

| City | Distance to Pittsburgh | Estimated Drive Time |
|------|----------------------|---------------------|
| Chicago, IL | ~300 miles (NE via I-90) | ~4.5–5 hours |
| Boston, MA | ~640 miles (SW via I-90/I-84) | ~10–11 hours |
| Atlanta, GA | ~730 miles (NW via I-75/I-71) | ~11–12 hours |

Pittsburgh's location at the western end of the Appalachian corridor puts it roughly at the center-of-gravity for these three teams, with the best highway/rail infrastructure.

### Recommended Venue: Carnegie Library of Pittsburgh

Pittsburgh has an excellent public library system with meeting rooms across multiple branches:

### Suggested First Meeting Details

- **Venue**: Carnegie Library of Pittsburgh - Allegheny (1230 Federal Street, Pittsburgh, PA 15212)
- **Date**: TBD (suggest 2–3 weeks from start)
- **Time**: 10:00 AM – 2:00 PM EST (allows virtual participants to join afternoon hours)
- **Format**: In-person with Zoom hybrid option
- **Agenda**:
  1. Project kickoff & introduce team leads
  2. Review clinical trial landscape
  3. Assign volunteer task categories
  4. Discuss event calendar for each city
  5. Coordinating logistics & procurement

### Virtual Backup

If travel is not feasible for all coordinators, quarterly meetings can be held virtually via Zoom. Use the weekly standup (Fridays 3 PM EST) for ongoing coordination.
