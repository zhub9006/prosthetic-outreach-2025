# Volunteer Task Coordination

This file tracks all volunteer tasks across the prosthetic outreach initiative teams.

## Task Categories

- **Research & Development** — Affordable prosthetic design, materials, and testing
- **Community Outreach** — Event planning, education, awareness campaigns
- **Logistics & Supply Chain** — Device collection, refurbishment, distribution
- **Clinical Partnerships** — Hospital/clinic coordination, patient assessment
- **Fundraising & Grants** — Funding applications, sponsor management
- **Volunteer Management** — Onboarding, scheduling, training
